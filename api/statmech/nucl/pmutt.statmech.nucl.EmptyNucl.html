pmutt.statmech.nucl.EmptyNucl¶

class pmutt.statmech.nucl.EmptyNucl¶

Bases: pmutt._ModelBase

Nuclear modes. Assumes no change in any chemical reaction and hence does not affect thermodynamic quantities.
